Practice Areas

Our clients' bottom line is our top priority. We are in constant communication with the people we serve and are committed to advancing the immediate needs and wants of our clients. Our team works around the clock to provide the necessary services and support that each client needs from us.

Civil Law

Civil law involves legal disputes between individuals, organizations, or businesses that do not involve criminal charges. These disputes may involve contract disputes, property ownership disputes, personal injury claims, or other civil matters.

Criminal Law

Criminal law involves legal disputes that arise when a person or entity is accused of a crime. This area of law covers a wide range of criminal offenses, such as assault, theft, drug offenses, and more.

Personal Injury

Represents individuals who have been injured in accidents caused by the negligence of others, such as car accidents, slip and falls, and medical malpractice

Family Law

Handles legal matters related to family relationships, such as divorce, child custody, child support, adoption, and prenuptial agreements

Mediation and Arbitration

Assisting clients in resolving disputes outside of court through mediation and arbitration.

Tax Law

Assisting individuals and businesses in navigating the complex world of tax laws and regulations.

Estate Planning

Helping individuals plan for their future and the future of their family by creating wills, trusts, and other estate planning documents.

Intellectual Property

We understand the value of intellectual property and work diligently to protect our clients' valuable assets and innovations.

Real Estate Law

We have extensive experience in real estate law and provide our clients with effective and efficient representation in all types of real estate transactions and disputes.

Sports and Entertainment Law

Advising clients in legal matters related to the sports and entertainment industry, such as contracts and intellectual property.

Employment Law

Advising employees and employers on workplace-related legal issues, such as discrimination, wrongful termination, and harassment.

Bankruptcy Law

Assisting individuals and businesses in filing for bankruptcy and managing the legal implications of doing so.

Banking and Finance Law

Advising clients on legal issues related to banking and finance, such as loan agreements and debt collections.

International Law

Helping clients navigate legal issues related to international business, trade, and treaties.
